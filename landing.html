About Us

Briko City is a home grown Newark clothing brand. Our goal is to develop new and exciting clothing with our city in mind. Given that the city of Newark is on its way to becoming the next Silicon Valley, the new class of young professionals are going to need modern yet elegant clothing inspired by the energy of this amazing historic city.
